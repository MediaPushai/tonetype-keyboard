## Brand Voice Reminders

**DO:**
- Be friendly and approachable
- Use contractions (we're, you'll)
- Keep it concise
- Include relevant emojis (but don't overdo it)
- Acknowledge the problem, offer the solution

**DON'T:**
- Sound corporate or robotic
- Use jargon or technical terms unnecessarily
- Be salesy or pushy
- Ignore negative feedback
- Post without proofreading
