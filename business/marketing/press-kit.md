## FAQ for Press

**Q: How is ToneType different from Grammarly or other writing tools?**
A: Grammarly focuses on grammar and clarity. ToneType focuses on emotional toneâ€”understanding how your message will be perceived emotionally and enhancing it with emojis and styling to match your intent.

**Q: Does ToneType read everything I type?**
A: No. ToneType only analyzes text when you tap the enhance button. We never log keystrokes, store messages, or monitor typing.

**Q: Why does ToneType need "Full Access" on iOS?**
A: iOS requires Full Access for keyboard extensions to make network calls. This permission does NOT give us access to passwords or sensitive fields, and we don't use it to monitor typingâ€”only to process text when you explicitly tap enhance.

**Q: Is ToneType free?**
A: Yes. The free tier includes 10 enhancements per day. Pro users get unlimited enhancements for $4.99/month.

**Q: What AI does ToneType use?**
A: ToneType uses OpenAI's language models for tone analysis. Users can also provide their own API key for unlimited usage.
