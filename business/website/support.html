Getting Started

How do I install ToneType?

iOS:

  1. Download ToneType from the App Store
  2. Open the ToneType app
  3. Go to Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Keyboard ‚Üí Keyboards
  4. Tap "Add New Keyboard" and select ToneType
  5. Tap ToneType and enable "Allow Full Access"

Android:

  1. Download ToneType from Google Play
  2. Open the ToneType app
  3. Tap "Enable Keyboard" and follow the prompts
  4. Select ToneType as your default keyboard

Using ToneType

How do I enhance a message?

  1. Type your message as normal
  2. Tap the ‚ú® button on the keyboard
  3. Wait briefly for AI analysis
  4. Your enhanced message appears with emojis and styling

Why does ToneType need "Full Access"?

Full Access allows ToneType to connect to our AI service over the internet. Without it, we can't analyze your tone.

What Full Access does NOT do:

  • It does NOT give us access to passwords
  • It does NOT log your keystrokes
  • It does NOT send data without you tapping enhance

Can I use ToneType offline?

Yes! ToneType includes basic offline tone detection. It's not as accurate as our AI, but it works without an internet connection.

Troubleshooting

Enhancement isn't working

Check the following:

  • Full Access enabled? Go to Settings ‚Üí Keyboard ‚Üí ToneType ‚Üí Allow Full Access
  • Internet connection? ToneType needs network access for AI analysis
  • API key valid? If using your own key, verify it's correct

The keyboard doesn't appear

  1. Make sure you downloaded ToneType from the App Store/Play Store
  2. Add ToneType in your keyboard settings
  3. Try restarting your device

I'm hitting the daily limit

Free users get 10 enhancements per day. To get unlimited:

  • Upgrade to ToneType Pro ($4.99/month)
  • Or add your own OpenAI API key in Settings
